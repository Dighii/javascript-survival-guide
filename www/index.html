Apple Cinnamon Energy Bars

These all-natural energy bars are made with the finest ingredients. Starting with a base of fresh McIntosh apples, we layer-in just the right amount of fruits and nuts. Using honey instead of sugar and omitting chocolate, we leverage all-natural sweeteners. These bars are not-only an excellent source of protein, but Omega-3 fatty acids (also found in green vitamins and fish). The ingredients are simple, and the preparation steps couldn’t be easier. Once you have finished, you’ll have a delicious way to start your day!

Ingredients

  • 1 McIntosh apple
  • 1 cup dates
  • 3 cups oats
  • 1/3 cup almonds
  • 1 tablespoon cinnamon

Preparation

  • Crush apples, dates and oats
  • Mix crushed ingredients
  • Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes
  • Let cool for 15 miunutes
  • Slice bars when room temperature
